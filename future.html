Technologically speaking, it is unlikely that the polaroid camera will advance much further than it already has. This is because the instant camera is popular due to its nostalgic qualities and physical product.

The Polaroid Camera gives people everything they crave today: instant gratification--they can hold what they captured immediately after taking it, a physical memory they can share directly with others, and a way to tap into the feeling of nostalgia everyone is experiencing in recent years.

As for design, the Polaroid Camera will provide designers with a way to add a physical, hand-crafted quality to their work whether it be for a book cover design, website design, or whatever else.
